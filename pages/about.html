ABOUT

Mothers of Matriarchy (MoM) is an NFT project that aims to celebrate the diversity and strength of mothers from around the world. The project features a series of artwork, each depicting a different mother from a variety of backgrounds and cultures. These images honor the unique beauty of each mother and highlight the resilience of motherhood. Our mission is to create a community that empowers mothers through the medium of NFT art, and to inspire and uplift mothers everywhere. We believe in the value of motherhood and aim to reflect that through our project.

 

Aside from the artwork, this NFT project will build a community platform where mothers, and those that support mothers, can connect with each other, share their stories, support one another, and provide allyship. Motherhood is far too often a thankless and underappreciated responsibility, but the MoM project looks to create a platform to not only celebrate the diverse spectrum of mothers and the important role they play in our lives, but also to create a supportive community where people can connect, learn, and grow together. By empowering and providing greater appreciation of mothers, we believe we can help create a more positive and equitable world for everyone.
