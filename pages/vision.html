VISION & VALUES

Motherhood

At MoM, our mission is to celebrate and empower mothers from all walks of life. We believe in the inherent value and resilience of motherhood, and we aim to create a supportive community that inspires and uplifts mothers and allies everywhere.

Diversity & Inclusion

Through our artwork, we aim to showcase the unique beauty and strength of mothers from diverse backgrounds and different parts of the world. We believe that motherhood is a journey that is enriched by cultural diversity and shared experiences, and we hope that our project will help foster a greater appreciation and understanding of the complexity of motherhood.

Community & Allyship

We strive to empower mothers and to create a community that promotes allyship and support. We believe that mothers are powerful and capable, and that they deserve to be celebrated and uplifted. By honoring the diversity and strength of mothers, we hope to create a community that promotes connection and empowerment, and that helps mothers to feel seen, heard, and valued.
